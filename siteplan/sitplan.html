AlertDrive Site Plan

Individual Project for WDD 231

Site Name

AlertDrive

The name is short, professional, and clearly communicates the core function: keeping drivers alert and safe on the road.

Site Purpose

AlertDrive provides a web-based dashboard for drivers and fleet managers to monitor fatigue levels in real-time. It integrates data to provide visual alerts, track patterns, and suggest necessary breaks to reduce accident risks.

Scenarios

  • "I am a long-haul truck driver. How can I see if my current driving patterns indicate that I need a break before I hit a critical fatigue level?"
  • "I am a fleet manager. Where can I find a summary of my drivers' alertness levels over the last week to help optimize their shift schedules?"

Color Scheme

Selected palette for high visibility and reduced eye strain during night driving:

  • Primary (Background): #1A1A1A (Dark Gray) - Used for page background.
  • Secondary (Text): #FFFFFF (White) - Used for primary body text.
  • Accent (Alerts): #FFD700 (Safety Gold) - Used for headings and warnings.
  • Critical: #FF4C4C (Soft Red) - Used for emergency alerts.

Typography

Headings: Roboto (Sans-Serif) - Selected for clean legibility.

Body/Data: Lato (Sans-Serif) - Selected for clear data presentation.
